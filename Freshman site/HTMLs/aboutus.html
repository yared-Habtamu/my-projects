We Are Here To Make Freshmans

Endless Academy prepares high school students for admission to the world's top universities with partial or full scholarships. Freshman Academy was founded in 2019 by a team of Yale-NUS students. Since then, Freshman's graduates were admitted to Harvard, Yale, Princeton, Columbia, Stanford, Berkeley, Babson, and many more. Our mission is not just to prepare you for IELTS and SAT or help you write a motivational essay. At Freshman, we are nurturing the next generation of world leaders in Politics, Economics, Humanities, Arts, and Sciences. Welcome to Freshman!
